# Issues (tactical)

Tracks incidental issues discovered in **documentation**, **code**, or **user account of UX**. These are tactical issues or bugs that exist in the current implementation. They may eventually be resolved by strategic backlog work; until then they are recorded here.

**Order:** By priority—impact, low effort, time until a strategic fix resolves them, user complaints (raise priority), etc. Top = highest priority.

**When to fix:** When an issue has **proximity to the current iteration** (same area of code/docs), or when the **user has complained** (priority elevated). In general, include a **small number** of issues in an iteration as a late addition; do not overload an iteration with issues.

**Distinct from BACKLOG:** BACKLOG = strategic intent, convergence of implementation on ideal design. ISSUES = tactical items that are true bugs or friction in the existing state.
